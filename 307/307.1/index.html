Nightwish

Introduction

Nightwish, a symphonic metal band hailing from Kitee, Finland, was formed in 1996 by Tuomas Holopainen, Emppu Vuorinen, and Tarja Turunen.

Background information

  • Origin: Kitee, Finland
  • Genere: Symphonic metal
  • Years active: 1996-present
  • Current main vocalist: Floor Jensen
